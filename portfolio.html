For our second group project, my three colleagues and I put together the World Famous Sports Encyclopedia, which offers sports fans valuable information about their favorite teams.

Trending News

Trending News offers users the top trends on Twitter to find relevant news items — germane to each trending topic — from CNN and Fox News

Users can enter his or her own search query and see what each news organization has written about it recently

Built as part of a group project with colleagues from UNH Bootcamp
